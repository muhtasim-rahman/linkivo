Privacy Policy

Effective: January 1, 2026 · Last updated: March 2026

TL;DR — We store your links in Firebase under your account. We don't sell your data. We don't show ads. You can delete everything at any time.

1. What we collect

Linkivo collects the minimum data needed to provide the service:

  • Account information: email address, display name, and profile photo (if signing in with Google)
  • Links you save: URL, title, favicon, metadata, and any notes you add
  • Usage data: open counts, like/dislike state, and browsing history within the app
  • App settings: theme preference, folder structure, sort preferences

2. How we use your data

Your data is used solely to provide Linkivo's features:

  • Sync your links and folders across devices via Firebase
  • Power the random discovery and point-weighting system
  • Enable folder PIN lock and app lock features
  • Provide browsing history within the app

We do not use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or use it for any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data storage

All data is stored in Google Firebase (Realtime Database and Authentication). Data is stored in the US region by default. Firebase's privacy policy applies to data storage.

Your data is protected by Firebase security rules: only you (authenticated with your UID) can read or write your data.

4. Link previews

When you open an embedded preview, Linkivo loads the URL in an iframe or fetches metadata via a proxy service (allorigins.win) to retrieve OpenGraph images. This means the target website may receive a request from your browser or from the proxy server.

5. Cookies & local storage

Linkivo uses browser localStorage to store your preferences (theme, sort order, etc.). No tracking cookies are used.

6. Your rights

  • Access: Export all your data from Settings → Export Links
  • Delete: Delete your account and all data from Settings → Delete Account
  • Portability: Export in JSON, CSV, Markdown, HTML, or PDF formats

7. Contact

For privacy concerns, please use the Feedback link in the app Settings. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
